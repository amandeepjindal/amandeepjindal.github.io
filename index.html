About me

I completed my Ph. D from IIT Delhi and B Tech from NIT Warangal from chemical engineering. I have been a research enthusiast and that is why, chose to go for research. My research topic in PhD was based on microfluidics, fuel cell and electrocatalyst nanomaterials. I was able to develop microfluidic fuel cells that used non-precious carbon nitride based nanofibers as cathode catalyst. My main motivation behind research career is to work for the sustainable growth of the society.
